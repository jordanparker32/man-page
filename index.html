- MyBanana
For our Software Engineering I course, we were grouped together and asked to come up with a project that could be marketed. We came up with the MyBanana project as we are all fairly active and wanted a better way to track nutrition. Most applications come bundled as an all-in-one solution to fitness and nutrition tracking, but they end up being quite bloated by the end. We wanted to create a simple application that would server severaly purposes.
The first reason is to address bloated fitness apps. We wanted something simple but still highly functional. We chose potassium and water as the main nutrient tackers as they are the main compounds needed for adequate muscle functioning. The second reason we made this app is to address the obesity epidemic in Mississippi. We wanted to create a fun and inviting user interface that would attract people that do not have any health or fitness knowledge, and give them an easy step in the direction of better health. Which is another reason why we chose to keep its simple with water and potassium. Switching from sugary beverages to water can elimanate a large portion of sugar in a persons diet leading to a plethora of health benefits.
